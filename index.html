Catchee

Social Impact Q&A Game Speed-meet

📋 The Problem

People experiencing loneliness lack tools that facilitate meaningful, low-pressure social interactions. Traditional social apps focus on matching but don't help break the ice or create genuine conversation starters.

💡 The Solution

Catchee is a speed-meet style Q&A game that pairs people for quick, timed conversations with fun and thoughtful questions. Players answer together, share opinions, and discover common interests - removing the awkwardness of meeting new people.

⚡ Speed-meet format 💬 Curated question bank 🎯 Points for matching answers 🤝 Low-pressure connections
