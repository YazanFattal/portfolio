Crow Studio

Game Design Social Connection Card Game

📋 The Problem

Loneliness is increasing among young adults who struggle to find meaningful connections. People find it difficult to open up and have deep, reflective conversations with others.

💡 The Solution

We created Kauwe Bende, a card game with the same concept as UNO but with questions that reflect on yourself. The game encourages players to share personal experiences, thoughts, and feelings in a fun, low-pressure way.

🎴 UNO-style gameplay 💬 Self-reflection questions 🤝 Builds genuine connections
